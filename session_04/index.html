<p class="right-text">


What if our garment could recognize and respond to the gaze of the other?<br>


Caress of the Gaze is an interactive 3D-printed wearable which can detect <br>


other people’s gaze and respond accordingly with life-like behavior.<br>


Our skin is constantly in motion. It expands, contracts and changes its shape


<br>


based on various internal and external stimuli including not only temperature <br> 


and moisture but also feelings such as fear, excitement and anger.</p><br>
